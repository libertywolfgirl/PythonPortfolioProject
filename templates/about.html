03 : About me

I'm a full-stack software engineer with 4+ years of professional experience building and shipping production web applications. My core expertise is in TypeScript, React, Next.js, and Node.js, developed primarily through my work at TransPerfect across several enterprise SaaS products. I'm comfortable across the stack, from RESTful and GraphQL APIs to relational and NoSQL databases (MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL), and I bring a strong testing discipline to everything I build, using tools like Vitest, Playwright, and Jest.

This project marks an extension of that experience into Python, where I applied the same engineering principles I use daily, clean architecture, thorough testing, and attention to real-world reliability, to build a server application from the ground up. It reflects my ability to pick up new languages and ecosystems quickly and apply solid software engineering fundamentals regardless of the stack.

I hold a B.A. in Politics and an M.S. in Criminal Justice, and I've built my technical skill set through hands-on, practical experience rather than a traditional CS degree, which is something I see as a strength, not a gap, in how I approach problems.
